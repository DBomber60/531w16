Syllabus and course outline

Office hours: to be determined (453 West Hall).

The course text "Time series analysis and its applications" (3rd edition) by Robert Shumway and David Stoffer is available for free online from the book's website.

Pre-requisites: STATS 426 (Introduction to Theoretical Statistics) or equivalent. For review, see "Mathematical Statistics and Data Analysis" by John A. Rice. Some matrix algebra is also required: For review, see http://www.sosmath.com/matrix/matrix.html.

We will be using the R language for computations (see http://www.r-project.org). No prior experience with R will be assumed. Please ask if you have difficulties accessing or using this software.

The midterm and final projects will be carried out independently, on data of your choice. If you like, you can check out some links I have collected to sites with time series data.
